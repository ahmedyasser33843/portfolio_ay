About

I think in systems,
build in layers.

I'm Ahmed Yasser — engineering student at Zagazig University, on track to specialize in Communications and Electronics Engineering. My curiosity has never respected department boundaries.

Engineering pulled me in because I needed to understand how things actually work — not what the formula says, but why it exists. That curiosity pushed me into low-level programming, Linux internals, digital logic, networking fundamentals, and the mathematics underneath all of it.

My goal isn't to collect skills on a résumé. It's to reach the point where I can sit down with a hard problem, strip away the abstractions, and build something that actually works — software talking to hardware, every layer understood, nothing taken on faith.
